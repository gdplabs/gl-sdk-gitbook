# Introduction to GL Meemo

GL Meemo is a smart platform that assists you in managing your meetings more effectively. With GL Meemo, you can:

* **Record meetings** through Google Meet integration, real-time recording, or by uploading an existing file.
* **Transcribe conversations** into accurate, searchable text.
* **Summarize discussions** into executive summaries, key points, and action items.
* **Export results** in multiple file formats for easy sharing and documentation.

By using GL Meemo, teams can focus on the meeting itself, while Meemo handles the task of documentation in the background.
